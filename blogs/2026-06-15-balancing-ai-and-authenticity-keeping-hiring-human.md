Examines how AI is reshaping both sides of hiring while stressing the need to keep the experience human. Only 28% of candidates actively use AI for job searching (47% for resume writing, 37% for real-time interview prep), while recruiters use AI mainly for administrative tasks like job descriptions (41%), communication (41%), and marketing content (39%); 63% of candidates accept AI in hiring but don't want it fully automated. Recommends transparency about AI's role, clear acceptable-use expectations, governance frameworks, and emphasizing skill validation over polished resumes.
