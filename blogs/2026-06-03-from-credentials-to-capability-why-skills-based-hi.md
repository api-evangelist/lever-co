As AI makes it easier for candidates to fabricate resumes, hiring teams are shifting away from credential-based evaluation toward validating actual skills and capabilities. The article emphasizes distinguishing between foundational requirements and performance-driving differentiators, moving skills validation earlier in the hiring process. The author recommends starting with small pilot tests before scaling organizational changes.
