This post recaps two episodes from the "People Actually" podcast featuring recruiting and HR leaders. Episode 4 with Katy Flatau (VP of Global Talent at JumpCloud) emphasizes that exceptional candidates are driven by passion for solving meaningful problems, and that global hiring requires cultural adaptation rather than simply replicating domestic strategies. Episode 5 with Ritu Mohanka (CEO of VONQ) highlights that great leaders prioritize getting hires right over filling seats quickly and maintain close connections to customer problems through frequent direct engagement.
